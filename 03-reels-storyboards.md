# NUSAÉ — First 3 Reels Storyboards

Reels are the only meaningful organic discovery surface in 2026. The goal of these three is to:
- get on Explore (Reel 1, hook-driven)
- convert curious viewers (Reel 2, styled walkthrough)
- close the loop with social proof / behind-the-scenes (Reel 3)

**Specs for all three**: 9:16 vertical · 7–15 seconds · shot in natural light (window-side, late morning) · phone is fine.

---

## Reel 1 — "Try-on hook" · post first

**Title in your head**: *Three ways one piece changes everything*
**Goal**: stop the scroll within 1 second.
**Length**: 8–10s

| Time | Visual | Text overlay | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–0.5s | Tight crop on neutral tank top, you facing camera | `wait for it…` (small, top-center) | Trending soft cinematic — search "outfit transition" or "soft girl aesthetic" in IG audio library |
| 0.5–2.0s | Hand pulls Ophelia into frame, you slip arm in | (none) | beat drop |
| 2.0–4.0s | Cut to full-body, walking gently, Ophelia drapes | `Ophelia, in white` | continues |
| 4.0–6.5s | Quick transition to grey colour, same outfit base | `same piece. different mood.` | continues |
| 6.5–9.0s | Final shot in black + caption hold on screen | `effortless. modest. yours.` then `→ tap the link in bio` | tail-out |

**Caption to publish with it**

```
The lace, the drape, the way it turns nothing into something ✿

Ophelia in white, grey, and black —
your everyday layering piece, made simple.

Comment OPHELIA and we'll send the link 🤍

#modestfashion #everydayootd #lacecardigan #sgootd #malaysianmodest #ootdmodest
```

**Why this Reel**
- Transition Reels overperform on Explore.
- 3 colourways in one Reel = doubles as a colour-decision tool for buyers.
- "Wait for it" hook + soft audio = high replay rate, which the algorithm rewards.

## Reel 2 — "Styled 3 ways" · post day 3

**Title in your head**: *One Ophelia, three vibes — tell me which*
**Goal**: make every viewer mentally pick a favourite (= comments).
**Length**: 12–15s

| Time | Visual | Text overlay | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–1.0s | Mirror selfie holding Ophelia on hanger | `Ophelia · 3 ways` | Show full piece clearly |
| 1.0–4.0s | **Look 1** — over a slip dress, soft glam | `01 · the dinner` | Walking shot |
| 4.0–7.0s | **Look 2** — over linen tank + wide-leg trousers | `02 · the matcha run` | Outdoor or cafe BG |
| 7.0–10.0s | **Look 3** — over a fitted top + maxi skirt | `03 · the everyday` | Indoor, neutral |
| 10.0–13.0s | Cut between all three quickly | `which one are you?` | Final hold |
| 13.0–15.0s | Brand outro — NUSAÉ logo on cream | `comment 1, 2, or 3 ↓` | Cream background |

**Caption**

```
One piece. Three completely different days ✿

01 — over a slip dress for dinner
02 — over linen for slow mornings
03 — over a fitted top for the everyday

Comment 1, 2, or 3 — which are you wearing first? 🤍

Free size · fits UK 6–14 · SGD $XX · pre-order

#modestfashion #styling #ootdinspo #modestoutfit #sgfashion #malaysianmodest
```

**Why this Reel**
- "Comment 1, 2, or 3" is the single highest-converting CTA for Reels because it's effortless.
- Algorithmically, comment count is the strongest reach signal after share count.
- Educational + product = bookmarkable, so it lives in saves long after the feed has moved on.

## Reel 3 — "Behind the scenes" · post day 5–6

**Title in your head**: *How NUSAÉ chooses what to sell*
**Goal**: build trust and personality. People buy from people.
**Length**: 10–12s

| Time | Visual | Text overlay | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–1.0s | Pile of fabric / candidate pieces on a table | `we tested 7 pieces this month` | Top-down shot |
| 1.0–3.5s | Hand picks one up, feels fabric, holds to light | `only 1 made it through.` | Slow movement |
| 3.5–6.5s | You wearing the chosen piece (Ophelia) | `breathable. flattering. honest.` | Walking, soft smile |
| 6.5–9.5s | Closeup of fabric label, packaging, tracking sticker | `handpicked. handpacked. shipped worldwide.` | Detail shots |
| 9.5–11.5s | Outro — NUSAÉ logo + bio link | `↓ shop in bio` | Cream bg |

**Caption**

```
We don't design our own pieces.
We hunt for the ones that earn their place ✿

Every NUSAÉ piece passes 4 tests:
✿ breathable fabric
✿ flattering fit
✿ everyday wearability
✿ honest value

If it doesn't pass, it doesn't ship.
Ophelia did. 🤍

DM us anytime 💌

#slowfashion #modestfashion #ethicalfashion #handpicked #malaysianmodest #sgmodest
```

**Why this Reel**
- BTS / "values" content over-indexes on saves and shares.
- It pre-empts the "is this just dropshipping?" suspicion that any new IG store faces — by being honest about sourcing, you reframe it as curation.
- Drives bio-link taps because viewers want to "verify" the brand.

## Audio strategy

- **Reel 1**: trending cinematic / soft-girl audio. Search the IG audio library for tracks with **5K–500K** uses (under 5K = too obscure, over 500K = oversaturated).
- **Reel 2**: same trending audio family — viewers will start to associate the sound with NUSAÉ.
- **Reel 3**: voiceover (your voice) over a soft instrumental. Voice = personality = trust.

**Save audio early**: when you find a track that feels right, save it from another Reel — you'll see use-counts climb in real-time and can post your version while it's still on the up-curve.

## Filming kit (already on your phone)

- **Light**: north-facing window between 9am–11am or 4–6pm. No overhead light.
- **Tripod**: any cheap clamp tripod or just lean against a stack of books.
- **Editing**: CapCut (free) or IG's native editor. Both have the trending audio library.
- **Hooks** to bookmark: Search "outfit transition reels", "modest fashion reels", "soft girl outfit" — copy the hook structure (don't copy the audio specifics).

---

## Metric to watch

For the first 5 Reels: **average watch time / Reel duration**. If it's >50%, you've cracked the hook. Below 30%? Re-cut the first 1.5 seconds — that's where viewers leak.
