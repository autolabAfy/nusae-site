# NUSAÉ — Caption Template

Goal: each caption pulls a stranger from "scrolling" → "saving / commenting / DM'ing".
Old captions were aesthetic but didn't qualify the buyer or reduce friction. New formula:

---

## The Formula

```
[1] Hook        ← one short, image-anchoring line
[2] Promise     ← what makes the piece different
[3] Proof       ← fabric / fit / specifics
[4] Social      ← who it's for / how to wear
[5] CTA         ← ONE clear ask, varied per post
[6] Hashtags    ← 5–8, mix niche + geo
```

Keep total length **under 80 words**. IG truncates after 2-3 lines on mobile, so [1] + [2] are the only lines most people read.

---

Comment OPHELIA and we'll send the link 🤍

#modestfashion #everydayootd #lacecardigan #malaysianmodest #sgootd #modestoutfit

**Why this works**
- Hook is 6 words; works as both first line and image alt-text.
- Specs answer the three silent questions every buyer has (fabric, fit, sizes).
- "Comment OPHELIA" triggers IG's algorithm (engagement) AND auto-replies via ManyChat or DM keyword automation.
- Hashtags: 4 niche + 2 geo. Keep under 10.

## CTA bank — rotate, don't repeat

Replace the current "💌 DM to order" on every post. Pick one per post, vary across the week:

| CTA | Algorithmic effect | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| `Comment OPHELIA for the link` | Triggers comments + DM auto-reply | Product hero post |
| `Save this for outfit inspo` | Saves boost reach > likes do | Styling / lifestyle |
| `DM SIZE to check stock` | Direct intent | Restock / limited |
| `Tag a friend who needs this` | Shares = strongest reach signal | Soft / wholesome moments |
| `Comment 🤍 if you'd wear this` | Easy reply = comment count | Mid-funnel / aesthetic |
| `What colour first — black or grey?` | Polls in caption format | Carousel decision posts |
| `Tap the link in bio to shop` | Drives to website | Once you have the site live |

## Hashtag bank

Mix 5–8 per post: 3 niche + 2 geo + 1–2 lifestyle.

**Niche** (modest fashion, lace, layering)
`#modestfashion #modestoutfit #modeststyle #modestclothing #lacecardigan #lacekimono #everydayootd #ootdmodest`

**Geo** (SG / MY focus)
`#sgfashion #sgootd #sgmodest #malaysianmodest #malaysiafashion #kualalumpurfashion #singaporestyle`

**Lifestyle / vibe**
`#softgirlaesthetic #quietluxury #effortlessstyle #cleanaesthetic #neutralwardrobe #slowfashion`

**Branded** (always include)
`#nusae #shopnusae #nusaecollection`

⚠️ **Avoid**: banned/spammy tags like `#followforfollow`, `#l4l`, `#instagood` — they trigger shadow-bans for new accounts.

## Cadence

- **Posts**: 3–4 per week (feed)
- **Reels**: 2–3 per week (see storyboards)
- **Stories**: daily — packing parcel, restock teaser, customer DM screenshot, BTS sourcing
- **Broadcast Channel**: 1x weekly when you have new pieces

A new account needs **volume** for the algorithm to figure out who to show you to. Less than 3 posts/week = invisible.
