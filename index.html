— FAQ —

A little guide before you order

How much do your pieces cost?

Each piece is priced individually and shown on the product. Ophelia is SGD $35. We keep prices simple. Free shipping for SG & MY orders.

Where do you ship from, and how long does it take?

We operate between Malaysia and Singapore and ship out accordingly. Most pieces are pre-order: processing 5–10 days, and shipping varies by destination. We will be updating your order progress via WhatsApp. This is to ensure transparency and clear communication with our customers.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes — we ship anywhere. International rates are calculated at checkout via DM.

Where do you source your pieces?

We don't design our own pieces. We handpick from trusted suppliers and platforms based on fabric, fit, everyday wearability, and value. If a piece doesn't pass our own wear-test, it doesn't make it here.

How do I order?

Tap any "Order on WhatsApp" button on this page. You can also DM us on Instagram @shopnusae. We reply daily.

What if it doesn't fit?

Reach out within 3 days of receiving your piece — we'll work something out. Because pieces are pre-order, returns are case-by-case. We'll always be fair.
