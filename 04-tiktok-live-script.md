# NUSAÉ TikTok Live Script Framework — Ophelia Outerlace

A repeatable script for Natalia's TikTok Live sessions selling the Ophelia Outerlace to Singapore + Malaysia. Built from analysis of the 49-min live on [date], targeting higher conversion while staying inside TikTok Live's rules.

**Use this every session. Record. Note what worked. Refine the script — not the format.**

---

## 1. What we learned from the last live

### What worked
- Asking "Are you from Singapore or Malaysia?" — opens conversation, lets us route the contact channel.
- Use-case stacking — Hari Raya, office, dating, dinner & dance, bridesmaid, beach, honeymoon. Every viewer sees themselves in one.
- Free-size reassurance for petite (53kg, 154cm proof) + plus size + hijab wearers.
- Founder story — affordable, comfortable, versatile.
- Styling demos — pin-up = kebaya look; fold = abaya look; layered over white innerwear.
- Urgency hook — Hari Raya Haji delivery deadline.
- Care tips (don't iron, steam only) — builds trust before purchase.

### What got us in trouble (TikTok flagged the live)
- **Said "WhatsApp" aloud** → restriction notification at ~36:00. Account had a prior restriction since 10.03.
- **Read out phone numbers digit-by-digit** → "972-290-61" — phone numbers are flagged as off-platform contact.
- **Said "DM me"** and **"Facebook"** — both platform names trigger filters.
- Coded workarounds ("wawa", "wawami") were better but inconsistent — flipped back to the W-word under pressure.

### What we missed
- No clear opener / value-prop / objection / CTA structure — just freestyled.
- No firm upsell sequence ("If I want $30, I'll be at $85" — Emin's note).
- No reply template — sending from personal phone, not the NUSAÉ business line.
- No share-the-live ask to friends/family early — only realised at the end that shared traffic boosted views to 700+.
- No scarcity / stock counter to push fence-sitters.

## 2. TikTok Live banned words & safe swaps

TikTok Live's algorithm auto-mutes / suppresses streams that mention off-platform commerce, contact details, or absolute claims. Keep this card open beside the camera.

| ❌ Never say (verbal or overlay) | ✅ Say this instead |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp / DM / Telegram / Messenger | "the chat app", "the green app", "our private line", "drop a hi at the link in profile" |
| Facebook / Instagram / Shopee / Lazada | "our other home", "the photo app" |
| Phone numbers (e.g. "9722 9061") | "link in profile" / "tap our profile, the number is pinned" |
| "Click the link" / "Link in bio" (overused → suppressed) | "Profile" / "Tap our name, it's there" |
| Best / cheapest / number 1 / only / 100% / guaranteed | "one of our most-loved", "really hard to beat", "we stand behind it" |
| Free / Giveaway (sometimes flagged) | "complimentary", "on us" |
| Cure / heal / treat / cures rashes etc. | (don't make health claims at all for fashion) |
| Buy / sell (overused → flagged in some regions) | "take it home", "yours today", "secure yours" |
| Specific currency symbols repeated ("$30 $30 $30") | Vary: "thirty", "three-zero", "the live price" |

### How to point viewers to your WhatsApp (without saying WhatsApp)
1. **Bio link is king.** Linktree → WhatsApp deeplinks for both numbers (SG + MY). All "go to profile" mentions push there.
2. **Emin pins comments**: `📩 SG ladies → tap our profile, link 1` and `📩 MY ladies → tap our profile, link 2`. He pins, unpins, re-pins every 3-4 mins to surface for new viewers.
3. **Overlay graphic** on the live: "TAP OUR PROFILE → ORDER". No numbers shown.
4. **Code phrases** to use verbally:
   - "Tap our name at the top, everything you need is pinned."
   - "Emin will drop the link — comment 'ME' and he'll send you over."
   - "The order link lives in our profile, sayang."
5. **Comment-triggered DM** (when supported): "Comment OPHELIA and we'll slide into your messages."

## 3. The 6-segment Live framework

Every live runs the same 6 segments on a loop. Target session: **45–60 minutes**. The loop runs every **10–12 minutes** so new viewers always catch a full cycle.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LOOP (repeat every 10–12 min for the whole session)     │
│                                                         │
│  1. HOOK & WELCOME             (60–90 sec)              │
│  2. VALUE PROP / FOUNDER STORY (60 sec)                 │
│  3. STYLING DEMO + PAIRINGS    (4–5 min)                │
│  4. OBJECTION HANDLING         (90 sec)                 │
│  5. OFFER + UPSELL             (60 sec)                 │
│  6. CTA + ENGAGEMENT BOOST     (60 sec)                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## 4. The Script

> **Convention**: `[BRACKETS]` = action / pause. `{curly}` = swap per loop. **Bold** = say this exactly.

### SEGMENT 1 — HOOK & WELCOME (60–90 sec)

> **"Hi babes, welcome to the live! Quick one — drop in the comments, are you from Singapore or Malaysia? I want to know who's with me so I can take care of you properly."**
>
> [Wait 5-10 sec, read names aloud as they come in]
>
> **"Hi {Name}, you're from {SG/MY} — got you, sayang. Emin is pinning the order link for both Singapore and Malaysia girls right now, so do tap our profile at the top, all the info is there, okay?"**
>
> **"For those just joining — I'm Natalia, founder of NUSAÉ, and tonight I'm showing you our Ophelia Outerlace. One piece, four colours, free size, and you'll see in a minute why every wardrobe needs one."**

**Hook variations to A/B test**:
- Curiosity: "Tonight I'm showing you the one piece that made my mum order three of."
- Urgency: "Hari Raya is {X} days away — let me show you the cover-up that'll arrive in time."
- Identity: "If you've ever stood in front of your wardrobe and said 'I have nothing to wear' — this is for you."

### SEGMENT 2 — VALUE PROP / FOUNDER STORY (60 sec)

> **"I designed Ophelia because I was tired of buying pieces I'd wear once and forget. Here's what I wanted — affordable, ladies. Lightweight, because our weather is crazy. And versatile — one piece, ten ways."**
>
> **"It's free size, but not the frumpy kind. I'm 154 cm, 53 kg, and you'll see it falls beautifully. If you're petite or plus size — Ophelia works."**
>
> **"And it's the kind of piece where you literally throw it over a basic top and jeans, and your whole outfit just lifts."**

### SEGMENT 3 — STYLING DEMO + PAIRINGS (4–5 min)

This is the **heart of the loop**. Each loop, show **one colour + two pairings**. Rotate so a 60-min live shows all four colours, each twice.

**Pairing menu — pre-stage these in the room**:

| Look | Inner | Bottom | Add-on | Occasion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Casual day** | White basic tee | Blue jeans | Sneakers / slides | Brunch, shopping |
| **Hari Raya pin-up** | Matching long-sleeve innerwear | Kain / long skirt | Pin Ophelia like kebaya | Eid visits, family |
| **Office elegant** | Plain blouse | White wide-leg pants | Heels, structured bag | Work, meetings |
| **Date night (black)** | Black inner | Black slim pants | Heels, gold accessories | Dinner, date |
| **Beach holiday** | Bikini / swimsuit | Linen shorts / sarong | Straw bag | Beach, resort |
| **Bridesmaid / event** | Slip dress matching colour | Same | Hair clip | Wedding, bridal party |
| **Abaya fold** | Long inner dress | — | Hijab + brooch | Modest events |
| **Honeymoon airport** | Tank | Wide-leg trousers | Tote, scarf | Travel |

**Demo script** (per pairing):

> **"Okay, look — this is the {colour} Ophelia. I'm pairing it with {inner} and {bottom}. Watch what it does."**
>
> [Put it on. Walk. Turn. Show the back. Show the tassel movement.]
>
> **"See how the lace catches the light? And the tassels move when you move — it's so soft, super lightweight. You're not going to feel hot in this, I promise."**
>
> **"This look is for {occasion}. So if you've got {specific event} coming up — this is the one."**

**Mini-demos to slot in**:
- **The pin-up**: "Watch — I pin it here, and suddenly it's a long kebaya. Totally different piece."
- **The fold**: "If I fold it like this, look — it's giving abaya energy. Modest, elegant, beautiful."
- **The drape**: "Or just let it fall open — boho, beachy, romantic."

### SEGMENT 4 — OBJECTION HANDLING (90 sec)

Cycle through these proactively — don't wait for questions.

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Will it fit me? I'm petite/plus size." | "Free size, but the cut is designed to fall — not hang. I'm 154 cm and it sits beautifully. Plus-size babes, it drapes over you, doesn't cling." |
| "Is it too sexy / sheer?" | "Layer a long-sleeve inner — match the colour and the detailing almost disappears into your skin. Totally modest, still gorgeous." |
| "Hijabi-friendly?" | "Absolutely. Pin it up and it becomes a kebaya. Fold it and it becomes an abaya. Made for hijab styling." |
| "How do I care for it?" | "Don't iron directly — it's delicate. Light steam, or iron with a cloth on top. Hand wash, hang dry. Travels well, doesn't crease." |
| "Will it arrive in time for Hari Raya?" | "Order tonight — delivery is 7–10 days, you'll have it in time." |
| "What if it doesn't suit me?" | "Tap our profile, message us first if you want — Emin or I will help you choose your colour." |

### SEGMENT 5 — OFFER + UPSELL (60 sec)

> **"Okay, the offer for tonight's live only — Ophelia is usually three-five. Tonight, one piece is three-zero. That's five off."**
>
> **"But here's where it gets good — two pieces is five-five. So you save ten on the second one."**
>
> **"My honest tip — pick a black and a beige. Black for evenings, dinners, dates. Beige for day. You'll wear both on repeat. Or — get the white for Hari Raya and the black for after."**
>
> **"Free shipping for both Singapore and Malaysia tonight. Yes, both."**

**Upsell ladder** — always offer the NEXT tier up:
- 1 piece → "for ten more, you get a second colour"
- 2 pieces → "if you're shopping for your mum or sister too, the third one is on us at a smaller markup" (only if true)
- Bundle suggestion: **"The Hari Raya Pair"** = white + beige. **"The Day-to-Night"** = beige + black.

### SEGMENT 6 — CTA + ENGAGEMENT BOOST (60 sec)

> **"Okay, here's how to order. Tap our profile at the top — Emin has pinned the link for Singapore ladies and the link for Malaysia ladies. Two separate links, tap the one for you."**
>
> **"While you do that — tap, tap, tap on the screen and share the live with one friend who needs an upgrade for Hari Raya. The more we share, the more we can do these promo prices."**
>
> **"And drop me a comment with your favourite colour — I'll style it on for you next."**
>
> **"If you've already ordered — thank you, sayang. Tag me when it arrives, I love seeing how you style it."**

1. **Pinning rotation** (every 3-4 min):

- [ ] Calculate conversion: `orders ÷ unique viewers`

- [ ] Update this doc → add what worked under Section 1

- [ ] Schedule next live, post a teaser on IG story


Three rules for refining:
1. **One change per live.** If you change the hook, opener, and pricing all in one session, you won't know what moved the needle.
2. **Refine the script, not the format.** The 6-segment loop stays. Words inside the loop evolve.
3. **Watch the recording back the next morning.** Watch at 1.5x. Note every dead minute. Cut it from the next script.
